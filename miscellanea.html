  • How does health informatics generate values to patients?
    • A few real-world examples in my very personal opinion (I claim no interests in the following companies.)
      Patientslikeme People connect to others with the same disease or condition and track and share their own experiences. Meanwhile they generate first-hand clinical observational data to help researchers, pharmaceutical companies, regulators, providers and nonprofits develop more effective products, services and care.
      TrialX Connecting patients to clinicial trials.
      Zocdoc Providing free online medical care scheduling service for patients by integrating information about medical practices and doctors' individual schedules in a central location.
      WeRx Comparing the prices of prescription drugs at local pharmacy stores and help cost-conscious patients save money.
      EpatientDave An inspiring cancer survivor and blogger who advocates for participatory medicine, personal health records and personal health data rights.
      Caringbrige Offers free personalized websites to patients so that they can stay connected with families and friend when facing serious medical conditions.
