About

Since our work on Health Research Opportunity Index (ROI) was published on Nature Biotechnology in August 2015, we have received overwhelming feedback, including many requests for data from both academia and industry. Therefore we created this site to illustrate Health Research Opportunity Indices (ROI) during 2000-2011 using an interactive visualization tool. We are still working on improving our model and will post future updates on this site. Please stay tuned! (Last update: 10/11/2015)

Citation

Health ROI as a measure of misalignment of biomedical needs and resources. Yao L, Li Y, Ghosh S, Evans JA, Rzhetsky A. Nat Biotechnol. 2015 Aug 7; 33(8):807-11.

Interactions

  1. You can click on the bubble to view the data of a specific condition.
  2. You can also select a year from the colored legend on the left to view the ROI for a specific year.
